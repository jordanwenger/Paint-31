Hi there! My name is Jordan Wenger. I'm a web designer by day and an artist by night. I picked up digital painting a number of years ago but never commited to practicing much. This project has provided a systematic approach to exercising my digital art skills that not only gives me ample breaks, but also makes the journey fun!


The Paint 31 challenge is simple. Paint something every day during months with 31 days. It has to be completed within the day, NO EXCUSES! At the end of the year, you should have 217 creations to look back on.

If you have questions or would like to join me for this challenge, please reach out to me at jordanwenger11@gmail.com
